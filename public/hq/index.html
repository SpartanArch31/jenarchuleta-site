The balance

Lead with the now (~65%)

Most content opens on her warmth, humor, or real life today — mom, dogs, faith, the small joys. That's the magnet that builds a loved, engaged audience.

The book + movie ride on top (~35%)

People follow her for who she is; they buy because of what she made. The story sells hard during launches — proud, never "trauma," never the daily identity.

The launch plan

Two launches, not one. Use the quiet window to load the gun; fire when it's all ready.

Now

Quiet phase (Kindle live)

Goal = reviews, not noise. Reviews are launch-day rocket fuel and take time to stack.

  • Personal ask: inner circle buys Kindle + leaves an honest review
  • Soft "available now on Kindle" on site + bios
  • Turn social back on with the hook → grow the list
  • Build the backlog + production kit in the background
When ready

Loud phase (all formats live)

Fire the grave hook to 200K with paperback + audio available and reviews already stacked.

  • Buy links → site + launch email
  • Launch broadcast + the desert Reel
  • Coordinated social campaign (grave day one)
  • Reviews push + ranking spike
Trigger to go loud: paperback + audiobook live AND the backlog/reviews are stacked. No calendar pressure — launch when it's built.
