🎬 Talking-head Reel — Wed
The script (teleprompter-ready, ~40 sec)
Okay, real talk for a second. For years I thought the big, loud, glamorous life was the one to want. The covers, the parties, the velvet ropes. And then I ended up here. A house full of dogs, shoes by the door, an ordinary Tuesday morning. And I keep waiting to feel like I settled… I never do. Turns out the good stuff was never the part people could see. It's the small stuff. The quiet stuff. So if you're in a season that feels small right now — don't count it out. Sometimes small is exactly where the good life was hiding. Okay. That's it. Go hug your dog.
How to shoot it
📱 Vertical (9:16) · phone at eye level · natural window light · look into the lens like you're talking to a friend · 30–45 sec · record 2–3 takes, keep the realest one · optional soft trending audio · no kids in frame, a dog is a plus.
